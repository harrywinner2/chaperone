How it works

I

The conversation

We call you. No forms, no profile. Just a conversation where we learn who you are — how you think, what you love, what you're ready for.

II

The introduction

When we find someone worth meeting, we send a quiet message. "Would you like to meet Jane?" You say yes or no. No pressure. No cost yet.

III

The arrangement

We handle everything. We find a time that works for both of you, send a calendar invitation, and remind you the morning of.

IV

The call

A private video call, the three of you. Your chaperone is present — not intrusive, but there. Helping you see the best in each other.
